The Black Brick Road of O.Z. is an ongoing interactive story/comic based on the Wizard of Oz. Art, story, and concept by the young Russian creator Xamag, brought to life with HTML5, CSS3 animations, and JavaScript by American cartoonist-turned-coder Rachel Nabors.
